What is the Racism?
- Discriminations or prejudices against different racial group,
usually caused by not understanding others difference, such as their appearence,
skin tones, traditions, etc.
Usual actions of the racists may contain disadvantages in daily life or even
some violations and also when the racism was even
more serious, in 19 century, they separated their bathroom and restaurants,
and even some seats in the bus.

What is the Reverse-Racism?
- Disciriminations or prejudices against the racial group
who usually have discriminates the reverse-racists' race.
Usually caused inside the countries or society of reverse-racists
or where there are more people who is in the society or group of the reverse-racists.
It usually caused because of the anger or the fear against the racists who treated them badly.
